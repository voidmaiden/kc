Instructions

Be the first to reach the finish line!
  1. The youngest player should go first.
  2. Start by flipping over a challenge card. Then complete the challenge on the card!
  3. After completing the challenge, you can flip over a "rhythm" card.
  4. Clap out the rhythm! If you clapped four times, you can move forward four spaces; if you clapped three times, three spaces, etc.
  5. The first player to reach the end wins the game!
